# Asset ideas — exploration only, NOT production

> Scavenged 2026-06-09 as a mood board for `docs/quality-bar.md`. Nothing in
> this directory ships. Nothing is imported from `src/` or `public/`. These are
> candidates to react to — keep/kill/replace decisions happen in a design spec.
> Open `index.html` in a browser to browse and listen to everything.

## Audio — synthesized originals (`audio/synth/`)

23 WAVs generated by `generate.mjs` (zero deps, `node generate.mjs`) — a
proof-of-concept of the "design our own, fully-owned tactile identity" route
from quality-bar §3.5. Covers the whole event inventory including a 6-step
combo pitch ladder (`combo-1…6.wav`). License: ours. These are sketches of a
*character* (dry, quiet, clicky) — real versions would be tuned by ear or in a
synth, but the generator shows the pipeline costs nothing.

## Audio — music placeholders (`audio/music/`)

- `junkala-retro-title-screen-cc0.m4a`, `junkala-retro-level1-cc0.m4a` —
  Juhani Junkala, [Retro Game Music Pack](https://opengameart.org/content/5-chiptunes-action),
  **CC0** (see `junkala-INFO.txt`). Transcoded from WAV to AAC for size.
  Energy-wise these are arcadey — probably wrong for tetra's quiet aesthetic,
  but they're the best-known CC0 game music and useful to test the audio
  pipeline against.
- **Finding: FreePD.com has shut down** ("Site Closed", verified 2026-06-09).
  `docs/quality-bar.md` recommended it as the lowest-risk free-music source —
  corrected there. Remaining CC0-ish routes: OpenGameArt (per-track licenses),
  Kevin MacLeod via incompetech (CC-BY or $30/track buyout), chiptune
  netlabels, HURT RECORD (the TETR.IO route), or commissioning.
- The real recommendation from quality-bar stands: tetra likely ships
  **no music or quiet ambient, off by default** (the Jstris/trainer pole);
  commission an original loop only if music becomes part of the brand.

## Icons (`icons/`)

Two complete candidate sets pulled for the same ~30 concepts (play, pause,
rotate, settings, keyboard, timer, trophy, stats, volume, target, undo/redo,
flame/zap for streaks, bot, grid, eye for ghost-piece toggle, …):

- `lucide/` — [Lucide](https://lucide.dev), ISC license. Geometric, 24px grid,
  2px stroke — the quieter, more neutral set. npm: `lucide-react`.
- `phosphor/` — [Phosphor](https://phosphoricons.com), MIT. Slightly rounder,
  more personality, 6 weights available. npm: `@phosphor-icons/react`.

Both are npm-installable so these SVGs are reference-only. Gut call to make:
Lucide reads more "instrument", Phosphor more "game".

## Images

- `images/reference/` — what the competition looks like, for side-by-side
  design discussion:
  - `tetrio-entry.png` — TETR.IO's entry screen (dark, dense, pixel-font
    chrome; note the ad slots we'll never have)
  - `jstris-home.png` — a **live Jstris multiplayer room**: six boards, the
    austere stats column (Time/Attack/Finesse/PPS/KPP), inline chat. The
    austere pole, warts and all.
  - `four-lol-home.png` — four.lol's docs design: calm cream background, big
    type, tetromino diagrams. Closest existing thing to tetra's "minimal,
    quiet, precise" voice.
  - `tetris-effect-steam-1..3.jpg` — Tetris Effect: Connected press shots
    (Steam). The maximalist pole: particles, full-scene reactivity. We study
    it for *escalation choreography*, not for volume.
- `images/sprites/` — [Kenney Puzzle Pack 2](https://kenney.nl/assets/puzzle-pack-2)
  (CC0): sample sheets, a few block tiles and white particle sprites — useful
  for prototyping particle effects and seeing rounded/connected block styles.
  tetra's real skin should be drawn, not stock; these are for mockups.

Quality-bar §5.4 note applies to all: preload + `document.fonts.ready` gate,
or bitmap-bake the in-game digits.

## What's deliberately NOT here

- Anything Korobeiniki-adjacent (trademark; see quality-bar §3.5 — never).
- Sonniss GDC bundles (pro-grade, free, RF — but ~30 GB/year drops; download
  on demand from https://sonniss.com/gameaudiogdc/ when we do the real SFX pass).
- freesound.org clips (per-clip license vetting; do it at selection time, CC0
  filter only).
- Block-skin art from the Jstris/TETR.IO skin communities — those are
  player-made for those clients, license-murky; inspiration only, via the
  screenshots.

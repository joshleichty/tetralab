# Vision

**tetra is a beautiful instrument for mastering competitive Tetris.**

Every feature exists to close the gap between the player you are and the player
who wins the 1v1. The training, the metrics, the drills — all of it serves
measurable improvement at modern guideline versus play: surviving and
weaponizing garbage, stacking efficiently, finishing faster. And because you
practice in this thing for hours, the craft of the app itself is non-negotiable:
minimal, quiet, precise. Quality is not a feature we trade against others; it is
the constraint everything else satisfies.

Everything spawns from this root. How the platform teaches — the modes, the
bots, the feedback — is open and will evolve. That it teaches, and that it is
beautiful, is not.
